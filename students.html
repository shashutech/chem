Dr. Akanksha Sharma

She belongs to Uttar Pradesh, India. She completed her Masters in Physics (2005) from University of Delhi and Ph.D. in Physics (2013) from School of Physical Sciences, Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU), New Delhi under the supervision of Prof. Subhasis Ghosh. She was the recipient of Junior Research Fellowship (JRF) and Senior Research Fellowship (SRF) from CSIR, New Delhi. She has more than 4 years of post-PhD experience including both teaching and research. She has extensive experience in fabrication and characterization of Organic Semiconductor based on thin film devices including thin film transistors, schottky diodes and solar cells. Her research interests include :

To develop an appropriate low cost environment friendly Solar Energy Harvesting System.

Electronic and transport properties of 2D systems and their applications.

To develop the devices based on spin transport
